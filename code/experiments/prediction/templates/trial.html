Instructions

In this experiment your task is to predict what will happen. A ball is dropped in one of three holes in a Plinko Machine (Figure 1). The ball comes to rest at the bottom of the machine (Figure 2). Your task is to predict where the ball will land (Figure 3).

The experiment works as follows:

  1. We will first ask you a few check questions to make sure that you've followed the instructions.

  2. You will then see a number of videos that show the ball being dropped in one of the three holes. This will help you get a sense for how the Plinko Machine works.

  3. Finally, you will see 122 images which show the initial position of the ball (like in Figure 3). Your task will be to guess where the ball will end up.

You will indicate your response by clicking with the mouse where you think the ball will end up. For each image, you will make 10 clicks. You can click on the same spot multiple times if you are confident about where the ball will land.

Please press Start to begin with the experiment.
